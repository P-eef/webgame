MIDNIGHT LODGING HOTEL

Welcome to the Midnight Lodging Hotel… once the scene of a brutal serial-killer massacre. The victims’ spirits roam the halls, confusing those who cross them for their killer.

Without a home to sleep in, you decide to reside in the fourth floor of the hotel. Unfortunately, an unexpected earthquake dropped debris and blocked off the stairs.

The ghosts now awake due to your presence, drift toward you with slow, relentless intent. Your only way out is the elevator, but the power is dead and the stairs lead nowhere.

Escape before time runs out.
